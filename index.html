The Introduction

What is The Magic Ministry?

The Magic Ministry is a specialty retail gaming store located in the heart of Melbourne's retail district. We specialize in the trading card game Magic: the Gathering and the role-playing game Dungeons & Dragons, as well as an assortment of board games and associated gaming supplies. Established in 2000, we have the knowledge and experience when it comes to the fore-mentioned games, hosting regular tournaments every week, catering to beginners and pros alike. We are a pure brick and mortar store, this website shows the majority of the content we sell, however we do not sell online and do not do any postal orders. Thus if your in Melbourne and looking for a great deal, pop into the store!
